Durga Puja Days 2022

Durga Panchami    30.09.2022

The day idols of Devi Durga along with her children comes to the Pandal.

Durga Shasthi    01.10.2022

Involves rites to awaken and welcome the goddess to be a guest, known as Bodhon.

Maha Saptami    02.10.2022

Bathing of the navapatrika with holy water done on the seventh day of the festival.

Maha Ashtami    03.10.2022

Sandhi Puja, involving the offering of 108 lotuses and lighting of 108 lamps.

Maha Navami    04.10.2022

The ninth day of festival is marked with the Homa (Fire oblation) and Bhog.

Vijaya Dashami    05.10.2022

The last day of this festival, called Bijoya Dashami is marked by Sindoor Khela.
